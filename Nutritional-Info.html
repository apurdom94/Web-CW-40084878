General

There are many aspects to living a healthy lifestyle alongside exercise nutrition plays a key role in this. Information on the internet is extremely vast and can be confusing. It is the aim of this section of the page to provide a broken down and easy to understand format. There is three key areas of nutrition that are essential to development. These are known as macronutrients. The three key ones are protein, carbohydrates and fats. It is a common misconception that if someone has too many carbs or fats they will gain weight. This is however not true as the only that contributes to weight-gain or loss is the number of calories consumed. If more calories are consumed weight will be gained and if less then weight will be lost. It is normal that people think this cause it is normally in the form of fat.

Calories

A measure of the energy content of food. Recommended 2000 per dat. For accurate assessment with the consideration of lifestyle and exercise you should consider BMR.

Protien

essential for muscle growth and development. Commonly found in meats. This is an extremely important if trying to loose weight as it allows us tp maintian muscles but is also very filling. It is reccomended to have around 1-1.5g per lbs of bpdy mass

Carbs

the most basic unit of energy. Provides energy for the body to use. The most common forms of carbs are pasta, bread, rice and potatoes.

Fats

although have negative connotations they are essential for the human body and are a form a secondary form of energy. Commonly found in white fish and other veg. differential from bad and good fats. We need to focus on ones like omega three.

Exercise

Exercise is also extrmely important to a healthy lifestyle. Even a walk each day can help burn calories - the guidance is around 10,000 steps a day but if you are looking to loose weight then any increase in steps will be of benifit. Weight training also has brilliant benifts if done properly with support of someone with technical knowledge. Alongside the physical benifits exercise has a whole host of benifits and is important for peoples mental health too.
