Ingrediants

  • 9 Chicken Sausages
  • 1 Tin Chopped Tomoatoes
  • Pasta of choice - 75g uncooked/person
  • 1 Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Seasonings - 2 tbl spoons paprika, 1 tbl sppon garlic powder, 2 tbl spoons chili flakes (or to taste)

Steps

  1. Fry off the chicken sausages until browned.
  2. Remove from pan and cut into small peices.
  3. Chop the peppers and onions and add to pan. Fry until softened.
  4. Add the saugsages back in along with the seasonings. Fry until cooked through
  5. Add in the tinned Tomoatoes and bring to a simmer. Simmer for at least 20 minutes.
  6. Bring salted water in a pan to a boil and cook pasta according to packet instructions.
  7. Drain pasta and mix in with sauce.
  8. Enjoy!
